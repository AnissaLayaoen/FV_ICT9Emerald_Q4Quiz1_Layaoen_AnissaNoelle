CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Crisostomo Ibarra

Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Crisostomo Ibarra is the main protagonist of Noli Me Tangere.
  • His family is wealthy, so he successfully finished his education outside of the country.
  • He has a liberal mind, possesses progressive ideas, and is outspoken that the antagonist, Padre Damaso did not like.
  • Rizal wants the young people during his lifetime to emulate the characteristics of Crisostomo Ibarra.

Maria Clara

María Clara de los Santos y Alba
  • Maria Clara is the beautiful and dutiful daughter of Capitan Tiago who has lived a sheltered life.
  • She is engaged to Crisostomo Ibarra and showed her love for him in the most unassuming ways.
  • She is portrayed as a trustworthy woman who has never lost her childlike innocence.
  • She possesses all the qualities that an ideal lady should have during the Spanish era.

Padre Damaso

Dámaso Verdolagas
  • Padre Damaso is a Franciscan priest who has lived with Filipino communities for almost two decades.
  • He is power-hungry and corrupt.
  • He is a shameless racist, petty, and spiteful.
  • He uses his power to make the lives of those who disrespected him miserable.
  • He represents the abuses committed by friars during the Spanish era.

Kapitan Tiyago

Don Santíago de los Santos
  • Kapitan Tiago is a wealthy Filipino and the father of Maria Clara.
  • He uses his wealth to build relationships with high-ranking members of the Catholic Church.
  • He is a hypocrite and tries to earn his salvation by paying others to pray for him.
  • He also participates in racial remarks directed toward his fellow Filipinos.

Crispin

Crispin
  • Crispin works as a sexton together with his older brother Basilio.
  • He was accused of stealing two pieces of gold by the senior sexton.
  • He was severely beaten because he failed to return what he was accused of stealing and eventually died.
  • Crispin stands for innocent people who have been falsely accused of committing a crime.
